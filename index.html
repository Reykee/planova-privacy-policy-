Privacy Policy

We believe your personal data belongs to you — and only you.

Effective Date: 23 February 2026
Planova is a fully offline productivity app. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you create stays on your device.

📋 Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Planova ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our iOS mobile application. Planova is a task management and goal tracking app designed to work entirely on your device without requiring an internet connection or user account.

By using Planova, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

🔒 Information We Do Not Collect

Planova does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Contacts, calendar events, or photos
  • Any content you create within the app (tasks, goals, notes)

We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking libraries.

📱 Data Stored on Your Device

All data you create in Planova — including tasks, goals, categories, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's local storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data will be permanently removed from your device.

🌐 Internet Access

Planova does not require an internet connection to function. The app does not make any network requests, does not connect to external servers, and does not sync data to any cloud service.

👤 No Account Required

Planova does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app. You can start using all features immediately after installation.

🔔 Notifications

Planova may request permission to send local push notifications to remind you of upcoming tasks and deadlines. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers or personal data transmission.

You can enable or disable notifications at any time through the app's Settings screen or through your device's iOS Settings.

👶 Children's Privacy

Planova does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for use by all age groups, as no data is collected or transmitted.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of Planova after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
