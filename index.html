Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you — and only you.

Effective Date: 23 February 2026
Planova is a fully offline productivity app. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you create stays on your device.

📋 Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Planova ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our iOS application. Planova is designed to function entirely on your device without requiring an internet connection or user account.

🔒 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Analytics or crash reports
  • Contacts, calendars, or photos
  • Any tasks, notes, or goals created inside the app

📱 Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. It is never transmitted to us or any third party. Deleting the app will permanently remove all stored data.

🌐 Internet Usage

Planova does not require internet access and does not connect to external servers.

🔔 Notifications

The app may request permission to send local notifications for reminders. These are generated entirely on your device.

👶 Children's Privacy

Planova does not collect any data from users of any age.

🔄 Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
